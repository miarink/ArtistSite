My recent concert experience

November 08, 2018 Posted by Jillian In : Music

  • 12k
  • 11.2k
  • 1.8k

The worst advice about music you could ever give someone would be that concerts provide the best music experience. That is not always the case as at some venues the music can be so loud that you cannot hear the details in it.

Not only can the loudness of the music detract from your experience, the noise from the crowd may do the same. In some scenarios this may not be an issue, such as electronic music concerts. At such concerts, crowd noise provides a lot of the energy and really adds to the experience.

But, in a lot of cases, however, music is best enjoyed in a comfortable environment with good equipment. Such environment, for me at least, would be my home. It's there that I have a very expensive dac/amp and headphone setup. I can relax and enjoy any music I want to listen to.
